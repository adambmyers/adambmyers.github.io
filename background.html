Originally from Northern Ohio, Adam worked his way to the top the burgeoning indoor waterpark industry. With a keen disposition for project management and an appreciation of streamlined operations, Adam opened some of the largest and most innovative operations in the world. One of the highlights of his past career is working as a safety auditor of the most prestigious aquatic facilities in the nation. The lessons learned in that capacity were numerous and are still relevant today.
Adam now brings the same dedication to quality product and eye for minute details to the development space. He has a passion for Java programming and a gift for front end development. A lifelong learner by nature, he enjoys consuming research of disruptive technologies such as 3-D printing, block chain, and artificial intelligence. He lives in New Albany with his cherished family and pets.
